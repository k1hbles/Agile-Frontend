Our Story

Since 1947, A Legacy of Trust

What began as a small biscuit maker has grown into a household name across generations. Khong Guan's signature red tin has sat on countless tables during festive gatherings, quiet teatimes, and everyday moments in between.

We still bake the way we always have — with quality ingredients, honest recipes, and a deep respect for the taste people grew up loving.
